Travel Info
Via HackMIT Bus
Arriving
You should have been contacted by your bus point already about when and where the bus will be picking you up from your school.

All buses will arrive at the Kresge Circle.
Departing
Buses leave friday afternoon at the following times:

University of Toronto - 2:30 PM

Columbia/Princeton/NYU - 4:00 PM

Brown/Yale - 4:00 PM

All buses leave from Kresge Circle
Via Plane
Arriving
Take the
Silver Line
towards South Station.

Once at South Station, take the
Red Line
toward Alewife and get off at the Kendall/MIT stop.

To get to Kresge Auditorium, follow the map on the maps page.
Departing
Walk to the T stop in Kendall Square and take the
Red Line
towards Ashmont/ Braintree.

Get off at South Station and take the
Silver Line
to Logan International Airport.
